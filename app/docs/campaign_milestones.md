# Campaign Milestone Progression (Rebalanced, 38–40 combats)

This document outlines the narrative and mechanical progression of the campaign from Level 1 to Level 11, using a milestone leveling system. Distribution emphasizes varied combat intensity (micro, skirmish, set-piece, boss) while preventing encounter bloat. Ember placement will provide rest cadence and reinforce dramatic beats.

---

## Act I — Shadows Over Greyharbour (Levels 1–3)
**Target Combats:** 5  
**Distribution:** Micro 3 / Skirmish 1 / Set-piece 1  

**Story Beats & Encounters:**  
- Outer Gate breach (fighter holds line).  
- Alley ghoul chase (rogue stealth route).  
- Ember shrine defense (cleric radiant counter).  
- First ember discovered as Greyharbour’s hope.  

**Companion Arcs:**  
- Fighter: weary veteran of many defenses.  
- Rogue: survivor of Greyharbour’s underbelly.  
- Cleric: moral compass, faith strong against encroaching necromancy.  

**Bosses:** *None.*

---

## Act II — The Necropolis Lands (Levels 3–7)
**Target Combats:** 10–12  
**Distribution:** Micro 3 / Skirmish 5–6 / Set-piece 2 / Boss 2  

**Story Beats & Encounters:**  
- Bone Bridge ambush.  
- Ossuary granary raid (cut supply of “soul-fuel”).  
- Slave pit revolt (choice-driven battle).  
- Lich emissary parley → betrayal battle.  
- Necropolis plaza set-piece with necrotic geysers.  
- **Boss 1:** Undead warlord commanding skeletal cavalry.  
- **Boss 2:** Bone-lord legate or lich adjutant testing the heroes.  

**Companion Arcs:**  
- Fighter: stoic in face of overwhelming empire.  
- Rogue: tempted toward selfish survival.  
- Cleric: shaken by blasphemies of empire.  

---

## Act III — The Strange Ascent (Levels 7–10)
**Target Combats:** 14–16  
**Distribution:** Micro 4 / Skirmish 7 / Set-piece 3 / Boss 2  

**Story Beats & Encounters:**  
- Crystal cavern leviathan worm (terrain collapse hazard).  
- Spore forest maze (hallucinatory skill challenge + skirmish).  
- Remnant clan rescue at ancient circle (choice: defend vs. flee).  
- Ruins haunted by echo-specters.  
- **Set-piece 1:** Cavern river crossing under shadow-beast assault.  
- **Set-piece 2:** Starlit chasm where fallen kingdom’s spirits test the party.  
- **Boss 1:** Ancient crystal guardian bound to necromantic seal.  
- **Boss 2:** **Guardian of the False Light** — radiant trap-monster, climax of ascent.  

**Companion Arcs:**  
- Fighter: resilience cracks, then hardens.  
- Rogue: cynicism proves useful.  
- Cleric: faith deeply shaken by false gateway betrayal.  

---

## Act IV — The Burning Return (Levels 10–11)
**Target Combats:** 7–8  
**Distribution:** Micro 2 / Skirmish 3–4 / Set-piece 1 / Boss 1  

**Story Beats & Encounters:**  
- Faltering teleport circle defense (ritual HP timer).  
- Necrotic storm battlefield.  
- Collapsing chamber jump (skill-heavy skirmish).  
- **Set-piece:** Last circle under siege, collapsing as the empire closes in.  
- **Boss:** Necrotic champion sent to bar the final portal — culmination of the empire’s wrath.  

**Companion Arcs:**  
- Fighter: Unyielding Stance — final embodiment of endurance.  
- Rogue: terror on battlefield, fearsome reputation realized.  
- Cleric: ultimate radiant stand (Reaper’s Shroud / Halo of Daybreak).  

---

## Totals
- Act I: 5 combats  
- Act II: 10–12 combats  
- Act III: 14–16 combats  
- Act IV: 7–8 combats  

**Total:** 36–41 combats (target ~38).  

**Boss Fights:** 0 in Act I, 2 in Act II, 2 in Act III, 1 in Act IV → **5 bosses across campaign.**
